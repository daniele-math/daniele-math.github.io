About me

I am a Lecturer at the Department of Mathematical Sciences, Stevens Institute of Technology, Hoboken, New Jersey, on the outskirt of New York City.



Research topics

Geometric Topology, Differential Geometry, Geometry of Surfaces.
Higher Teichmüller-Thurston Theory, Higgs Bundles, Geometric Structures, Character Varieties.
Teichmüller-Thurston Theory, Hyperbolic Geometry.
