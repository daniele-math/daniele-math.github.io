Registration

If you want to take part in the conference, please fill the registration form below by February 19th.

Accommodation: A majority of the participants will be hosted in the conference hotel, and the price for their room will be covered by the conference. Some participants may need to stay in a separate hotel, and pay for their room. We will book the hotel for everybody.

We also have some limited funding for reimbursing the travel expenses of some of the participants, please mark in the application form if you need this travel support.

If the application form does not work for you, please register by sending an email to one of the organizers. Similarly, for any questions, please send an e-mail to one of the organizers.
