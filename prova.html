About me

I currently work at the Mathematical Institute, University of Heidelberg, in Germany. I'm in the research group Differential Geometry, lead by Anna Wienhard.

Previously, I have been Post-Doc of CNRS at Institut de Recherche Mathématiques Avancée, Université de Strasbourg, France, for two years. There I worked mainly with Athanase Papadopoulos.

I did my PhD In Pisa, Italy, at Scuola Normale Superiore. I worked at my PhD thesis under the supervision of Riccardo Benedetti, and I defended my thesis at Scuola Normale Superiore on December 20, 2007. I also had a research grant of 9 month at the Department of Mathematics, University of Pisa.
