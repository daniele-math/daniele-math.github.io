GTiNY

Geometric Topology in New York

Columbia University, June 2nd-4th, 2023


Dates: June 2nd-4th, 2023.

Place: Department of Mathematics, Columbia University in the City of New York.

Organizers: Daniele Alessandrini, Jason Behrstock, Abhijit Champanerkar, Ilya Kofman, and Francesco Lin.


We will discuss several aspects of Geometric Topology, including

  • Knots and 3-manifolds
  • Geometry and Groups
  • Surfaces and Manifolds
The schedule will leave ample room for discussion and scientific interaction.
