My CV

Here you can download my CV.

Since September 2019 I am at the Department of Mathematics, Columbia University, New York.

During the period October 2012 - August 2019 I was working at the Mathematisches Institut, Universität Heidelberg, in Germany. I was in the research group Differential Geometry, led by Anna Wienhard.

During the year 2011/2012 I was at the Department of Mathematics, University of Fribourg, in Switzerland, in the research group of Ruth Kellerhals.

Before that I spent one year at Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in Bonn, Germany.

Previously, I have been Post-Doc of CNRS at Institut de Recherche Mathématiques Avancée, Université de Strasbourg, France, for two years. There I worked mainly with Athanase Papadopoulos.

I did my PhD In Pisa, Italy, at Scuola Normale Superiore. I worked at my PhD thesis under the supervision of Riccardo Benedetti, and I defended my thesis at Scuola Normale Superiore on December 20, 2007. I also had a research grant of 9 month at the Department of Mathematics, University of Pisa.
